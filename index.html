A dedication to surgical precision and patient-centered care.

Dr. James Caristo is a leading cardiothoracic surgeon based in Sydney, known for his meticulous approach to complex heart and lung procedures. His practice is built on a foundation of clinical excellence and a deep commitment to the well-being of his patients.

As a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS), Dr. Caristo brings decades of specialized training and experience to every case, ensuring that patients receive the highest standard of care available globally.
