The Surgeon

A dedication to surgical excellence and patient-centered care.

Dr. James Caristo is a leading cardiothoracic surgeon based in Sydney, internationally recognized for his meticulous approach to complex heart and lung procedures. His practice is built on a foundation of clinical mastery and an unwavering commitment to patient outcomes.

As a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS), Dr. Caristo brings decades of specialized training and experience to every case, ensuring that patients receive the highest standard of care available globally.
